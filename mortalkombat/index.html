Mortal Kombat

The Mortal Kombat series completely rejuvinated and boosted the 2D fighting genre to a whole new level of design. The first few MK games actually used mocap actors as the base for all of the characters which was unheard of at the time of MK's inception. While this game was revolutionary it also holds a special place in my heart as these games are a sort of bonding tool for me and my father. My dad would play these before I was born and one day while he was playing I was hooked on games ever since he asked if I wanted to play. We still play MK 11 when we both have the chance to get on and say how we're going to beat eachother only to lose like an idiot to the other player.
